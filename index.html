About

For many times I wanted to consistenly change the colors of stylesheets, but it always ended up being a tedious and large amount of work. I started searching for something and the only tool I found was this one. However, it has some bugs and the author doesn't seemed to be updating it very often. Also, I saw some room for improvement, so I decided to build a tool that would do that, and here it is! I think it could be useful for someone out there on this little planet of ours.

Feel free to share your thoughts and inform me about some bug that you may stumble upon. You can check out my website or talk to me on Twitter.

Requirements:
Any browser should work fairly well, but please update yours to the latest version.
